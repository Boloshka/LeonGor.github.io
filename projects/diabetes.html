Diabetes Risk Analysis

A data-driven investigation into the predictors of diabetes risk using patient health indicators.

Question

Which clinical and demographic factors are most strongly associated with elevated diabetes risk?

What I Did

Cleaned and profiled a structured health dataset, performed exploratory analysis, engineered useful features, and built a classification model to identify major risk drivers.

Key Findings

Glucose concentration, BMI, and age emerged as the strongest predictors. The model highlighted high-risk cohorts that could be prioritized for early intervention.

Why It Matters

The analysis supports proactive healthcare strategies by helping teams identify at-risk patients earlier and allocate preventive resources more effectively.
