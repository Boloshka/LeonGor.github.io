Critical Data Lineage for System Migration

Creating end-to-end visibility of critical data movement to de-risk a complex system migration.

Problem

Migration planning lacked clear traceability across upstream and downstream systems, making it difficult to estimate breakpoints, reconcile ownership, and sequence releases safely.

What I Did

I mapped critical entities across source systems, transformation logic, and consuming applications, then built lineage documentation and validation checkpoints that product and engineering teams could use.

Key Insight

Most migration risk sat not in primary tables but in low-visibility dependencies embedded in reporting extracts and manual handoff steps.

Impact

The program gained clearer cutover sequencing, stronger controls for data integrity, and fewer late-stage surprises during migration waves.
