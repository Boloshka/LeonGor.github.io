Proactive Network Issue Detection

Detecting network degradation earlier by combining telemetry patterns with operational incident context.

Problem

Network teams were often reacting after customer complaints, because existing alerts focused on isolated thresholds rather than patterns that signaled emerging service degradation.

What I Did

I developed a detection approach that combined historical incidents, device health metrics, and regional traffic behavior to identify high-risk conditions before formal outages were declared.

Key Insight

Multi-signal shifts over short intervals were a stronger early warning than any single metric breach, especially in high-load periods.

Impact

Operations teams improved their lead time for intervention, reducing customer disruption and enabling more targeted escalation decisions.
