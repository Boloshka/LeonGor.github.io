Hillstrom Marketing Analysis

Evaluation of direct marketing campaign impact across customer segments to guide smarter targeting strategy.

Question

How did campaign contact strategy influence conversion behavior across customer groups?

What I Did

Conducted campaign response analysis by segment, compared treatment outcomes, and examined incremental lift to identify where outreach generated meaningful return.

Key Findings

Conversion uplift was strongest in prior buyers with mid-to-high historical spend, while low-engagement segments showed limited incremental impact from broad outreach.

Why It Matters

These insights help marketing teams optimize targeting, reduce wasted spend, and focus communication efforts where the expected return is highest.
