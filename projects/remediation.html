Customer Risk Prioritisation Redesign

Reframing customer risk signals into a practical prioritisation model for faster action and clearer governance.

Problem

Case teams were reviewing customers in a mostly chronological queue, which made it hard to consistently identify the highest-risk profiles early enough for preventive intervention.

What I Did

I consolidated operational, behavioural, and historical risk indicators into a weighted scoring framework, then worked with stakeholders to align thresholds to existing remediation capacity and policy.

Key Insight

A small set of compounding signals explained the majority of severe downstream outcomes, allowing prioritisation to be both simpler and more accurate than the previous process.

Impact

Teams gained a clear, defensible triage approach that improved response speed for critical cases and increased confidence in risk-based decision-making.
